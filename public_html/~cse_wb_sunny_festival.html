Festival

Sunny Island hosts one of the biggest festivals you'll ever witness three times a year, one being in the Spring and two in the Summer. People from all over the world visit to attend this special occasion. The festival includes not only games and live performances, but also extras such as crops and fruit markets, bakeries, flower shops, art galleries, and the unlimited taste testing!

Festival History

Sunny Island festivals is celebrating it's 100th anniversary!The festival came into existence as a way to celebrate the finding of the island. A. Sunny founded Sunny Island by mere accident while sailing across the Bahamas. Since then, the island has developed into a beautiful vacation resort, while still holding the same traditions that were developed when the island was first discovered.
